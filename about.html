❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you’ve wondered about MeowMeow.

Is MeowMeow a store? Do you sell products?

Nope! We don’t sell anything. We’re a free guide that helps you find and compare the best products from real online stores like Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress.

How do you make money?

Some of our links are affiliate links. If you buy through them, we may earn a small commission — at absolutely no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the lights on and the content free!

Are your reviews biased?

Never. We purchase or test most products ourselves, and base reviews on real performance, customer feedback, and value. We reject paid placements and sponsored rankings.
