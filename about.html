Frequently Asked Questions

Does MeowMeow sell products directly?

No, MeowMeow is a product discovery and affiliate platform. When you click "Buy", you are redirected to the official store (Amazon, AliExpress, Temu, or Daraz) where you complete your purchase.

Do you earn a commission?

Yes. MeowMeow participates in affiliate programmes. We may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you when you purchase through our links. This helps us keep the site free.

Are prices accurate?

Prices are updated regularly but may change on the partner store. Always verify the final price before purchasing.

Use our Contact page to send us a message. We respond within 24 hours.
