Everything you need to know about MeowMeow

Home/FAQ

Is MeowMeow a store? Do you sell products?

No! MeowMeow is a free product discovery and recommendation website. We do not sell products directly. All purchases are made through third-party sellers like Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress — we simply help you find the best deals.

How does MeowMeow make money?

We earn small affiliate commissions when you buy through our links — at absolutely no extra cost to you. The price you see is the exact same price you'd pay shopping directly on Amazon, Daraz, etc. Our commissions come from the retailer's margin, not from you.

Do you handle shipping or returns?

No. All shipping, returns, and customer service are handled directly by the seller (Amazon, Daraz, Temu, AliExpress) from whom you make your purchase. Always check the seller's policies before buying. See our Shipping Info and Returns Guide for more details.

Are your reviews and recommendations biased?

Never. Our product recommendations are based on real customer reviews, verified ratings, and independent research. Affiliate commissions do not influence which products we feature or how we rank them. Our goal is to help you find genuinely good products.

How often are deals updated?

Our hot deals refresh every 24 hours. Our team monitors prices daily across all partner marketplaces. Some product categories are reviewed and updated weekly to ensure accuracy.

Can I trust the prices shown on MeowMeow?

We strive for accuracy, but prices on external marketplaces can change at any time without notice. Always verify the final price on the seller's official product page before completing your purchase. We are not responsible for price discrepancies.

Are the affiliate marketplaces safe to buy from?

Yes. Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress are all established, reputable global marketplaces with buyer protection programs, secure payment systems, and return policies. However, always read individual seller reviews within those platforms before purchasing.

How do I contact you if I have a problem?

For issues with a product or order, contact the seller directly through the marketplace. For questions about MeowMeow itself (broken links, suggestions, partnerships), email us at onlinemeowmeow@gmail.com or use our Contact Form.

How do I unsubscribe from your newsletter?

Every newsletter we send includes an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Click it to be removed from our list instantly. You can also email us and we'll remove you manually within 24 hours.
