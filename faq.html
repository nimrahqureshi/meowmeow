About MeowMeow

MeowMeow is an affiliate shopping site that curates the best products from top online marketplaces including Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress. We handpick products based on quality, value, and popularity, helping you discover great deals without the endless scrolling.

No, MeowMeow is not a store. We don't sell products directly. When you click a product link on our site, you'll be redirected to the retailer's website (Amazon, Daraz, etc.) where you can complete your purchase. All transactions happen on those platforms.

MeowMeow earns a small commission when you click our links and make a purchase on a partner retailer's site. This commission comes at no extra cost to you — the price you pay is the same whether you come through our link or not. This is how affiliate marketing works.

We do our best to keep prices current, but prices on partner sites can change at any time without notice. Always verify the final price on the retailer's website before purchasing. We update our listings regularly but cannot guarantee real-time accuracy.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping is handled entirely by the retailer where you place your order (Amazon, Daraz, Temu, or AliExpress). MeowMeow has no involvement in the shipping or fulfillment process. For shipping questions, please contact the retailer directly.

Delivery times vary by retailer and your location. Daraz typically delivers within 3–7 business days in Pakistan. Amazon Prime items often arrive in 1–2 days. Temu and AliExpress can take 7–20 days for international shipping. Check each retailer's estimated delivery date at checkout.

International availability depends on the retailer. Amazon, Temu, and AliExpress ship to many countries worldwide. Daraz primarily serves South Asian markets. Check your retailer of choice for availability in your region.

Returns & Refunds

Yes — but returns are managed by the retailer where you made your purchase, not by MeowMeow. Each retailer has its own return policy. Amazon has a generous 30-day return window for most items. Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress also have buyer protection programs.

Contact the retailer's customer service directly to report the issue and request a replacement or refund. Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress all have buyer protection policies that cover damaged or incorrect orders.

Affiliate Links & Privacy

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks whether a purchase was made after clicking from our site. It lets us earn a small commission from the retailer at no additional cost to you. The product price remains exactly the same.

Absolutely not. Using our affiliate links never increases the price you pay. In fact, we sometimes have exclusive discount codes that save you even more money. The commission we earn comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not from your pocket.

We select products based on customer reviews, sales data, value for money, and our own testing. We only feature products we genuinely believe are worth buying. While we do earn commissions, we never recommend a product solely based on commission rates — our readers' trust comes first.
