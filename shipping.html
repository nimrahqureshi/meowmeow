Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with your tracking number from the retailer. You can track your order directly on the retailer's website:

  • Amazon: Track via Your Orders in your Amazon account.
  • Daraz: Track via the Daraz app or website under My Orders.
  • Temu: Use the Temu app's order tracking section.
  • AliExpress: Track via AliExpress My Orders or use the provided tracking number on a courier site.

International Shipping

Most of our partner retailers offer international shipping. However, availability, costs, and delivery times vary by country. Some items may not be eligible for international shipping due to local regulations or the seller's policy.

Always check the shipping options available in your country at checkout before completing your purchase.

Free Shipping

Many products on our partner platforms qualify for free shipping. Look for the "Free Shipping" badge on product listings, or check Amazon Prime eligibility. Daraz often offers free shipping on orders above a minimum value.

We highlight free shipping deals whenever possible on our product cards to help you save more.
