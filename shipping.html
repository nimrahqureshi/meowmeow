🚚 Shipping Information

What to expect when ordering through our partner stores

Home/Shipping Info

Important Notice

MeowMeow does NOT handle shipping. All orders are fulfilled directly by the third-party seller (Amazon, Daraz, Temu, AliExpress). Shipping times, costs, and methods vary by seller, product, and destination region.

International Shipping

Most products on our partner platforms ship worldwide. During checkout, you'll see which countries are supported for each item. Some products may only be available in certain regions.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • 🇵🇰 Pakistan (Daraz): 3–7 business days
  • 🇺🇸 US / 🇨🇦 Canada (Amazon): 2–7 business days
  • 🌍 International (AliExpress / Temu): 7–25 business days
  • ⚡ Express shipping options are often available at checkout

Shipping Costs

Many sellers on Amazon, Daraz, Temu, and AliExpress offer FREE shipping. When free shipping isn't available, costs are calculated at checkout based on your location, item weight, and chosen shipping speed.

Order Tracking

After your purchase, you'll receive a tracking number via email from the seller. Visit their website and enter the tracking number in "My Orders" or the tracking portal to monitor your shipment in real time.

Customs & Import Duties

International customers may be responsible for customs fees, import taxes, or duties charged by their local government. These fees are NOT included in the product price and are the buyer's responsibility. Check your country's import regulations before ordering.

Delayed or Missing Orders

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated time or hasn't arrived, contact the seller directly through the marketplace's customer service portal. They have the tools and authority to investigate and resolve shipping issues.

For specific shipping questions about an order, always contact the seller directly through their website.
