🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here’s how we handle your data.

Home / Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MeowMeow (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website meowmeow.com.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our site, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect minimal data necessary to operate our services:

  • Email Address — Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter or contact us via our contact form.
  • Browser & Device Info — Automatically collected via cookies (e.g., IP address, browser type, OS, pages visited).
  • Interaction Data — Clicks on product links, time spent on pages, and referring sites (used for analytics).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for limited, specific purposes:

  • To send newsletters (if subscribed) — you can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To respond to your messages sent via our Contact Form.
  • To analyze site traffic and improve user experience.
  • To serve relevant affiliate ads and track clicks (see “Cookies” section below).

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and deliver personalized content and ads.

Cookies we use include:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for site functionality (e.g., keeping items in cart).
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies — Used by third parties (Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, etc.) to show you relevant affiliate product ads.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect site functionality (e.g., shopping cart won’t save).

5. Affiliate Disclosure & Third-Party Links

MeowMeow is an affiliate marketing website. When you click on a product link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from Amazon, Daraz, Temu, AliExpress, or other partner stores.

These third-party sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

6. Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address solely to send you updates, deals, and blog posts. We use a trusted email service provider (like Mailchimp or Buttondown) that also complies with privacy regulations.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of every email. We will promptly remove you from our mailing list.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU, California), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability or restriction of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: onlinemeowmeow@gmail.com

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to industry-standard protections.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: onlinemeowmeow@gmail.com

Website: https://meowmeow.com

Thank you for trusting MeowMeow. We’re committed to transparency and your digital rights.
