📢 Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency is everything. Here’s how we’re funded.

Home / Affiliate Disclosure

Last Updated: April 5, 2025

TL;DR: We may earn a small commission if you buy through our links — at absolutely no extra cost to you. This helps keep our content free, honest, and independent.

What Is an Affiliate Link?

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks if you click through from our site and make a purchase. If you do, the retailer (like Amazon or Temu) pays us a small commission as a “thank you” for sending them a customer.

You pay the exact same price — no markups, no hidden fees. In fact, sometimes we even find coupons or deals for you!

Which Programs Do We Participate In?

We are proud participants in the following affiliate programs:

  • Amazon Associates Program
  • Daraz Affiliate Program
  • Temu Affiliate Program
  • AliExpress Affiliate Program
  • ...and other trusted global marketplaces

Does This Affect Our Reviews?

Absolutely not. Our #1 priority is helping you find the best products for your needs and budget. We research, test (when possible), and compare real user reviews before recommending anything.

Commissions never influence our ratings, rankings, or editorial decisions. If a product is bad, we’ll say so — even if it’s from a major affiliate partner.

Why Do We Use Affiliate Links?

Running MeowMeow takes time, effort, and resources. Affiliate commissions allow us to:

  • Keep the site 100% free for you
  • Invest in better tools, research, and content
  • Stay independent — we don’t accept paid placements or sponsored reviews

Your Trust Matters Most

We built MeowMeow to be your unbiased shopping sidekick. If we recommend something, it’s because we genuinely believe it’s worth your money — not because it pays us more.

If you ever feel a recommendation seems biased or inaccurate, please let us know. We take feedback seriously.

Thank you for supporting us by using our links. It means the world — and helps us keep delivering honest, helpful content to shoppers like you.
