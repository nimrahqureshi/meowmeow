Home Affiliate Disclosure

The Short Version: MeowMeow earns commissions from qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links. This costs you nothing extra. We only recommend products we genuinely believe are worth buying.

1. What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing arrangement where a website (like MeowMeow) earns a commission by promoting another company's products or services. When you click a link on our site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the retailer.

This is a standard practice in online publishing. Millions of websites, blogs, and content creators use affiliate marketing to monetize their content.

2. Our Affiliate Relationships

MeowMeow has affiliate relationships with the following platforms (and may add more in the future):

  • Amazon Associates Program: We participate in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.
  • Daraz Affiliate Program: We participate in the Daraz affiliate program for markets in Pakistan, Bangladesh, and other South Asian countries.
  • Temu Affiliate Program: We may earn commissions through the Temu affiliate program.
  • AliExpress Affiliate Program: We participate in the AliExpress affiliate program through the AliExpress Portals platform.

3. Does This Cost You More?

No. Using our affiliate links never increases the price you pay. The commission we earn comes from the retailer's marketing budget. In some cases, we may even have exclusive discount codes that save you money. The price you see is always the same as if you went directly to the retailer's website.

4. How We Select Products

We choose products to feature based on:

  • Customer reviews and ratings on the retailer's platform
  • Value for money and price competitiveness
  • Product quality and durability based on available information
  • Popularity and trending status
  • Our own team's testing and experience where applicable

While we do earn commissions from the products we recommend, we do not allow commission rates to dictate our recommendations. We will never recommend a product we consider to be poor quality, regardless of the commission it offers. Our readers' trust is more valuable to us than any commission.

5. Sponsored Content

Occasionally, brands may pay for sponsored posts or featured placements on MeowMeow. Any sponsored content will be clearly labeled as "Sponsored," "Ad," or "Promoted" to ensure full transparency.

6. FTC Compliance

This disclosure is made in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR § 255), which require clear disclosure of material connections between endorsers and the brands they promote. Our affiliate relationships constitute a material connection that could affect the content we publish.

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we select products, please contact us at onlinemeowmeow@gmail.com. We're committed to transparency and will always respond honestly to your inquiries.
