Important Note

MeowMeow is an affiliate shopping site. We do not process returns or issue refunds directly. All returns and refunds are handled by the retailer where you made your purchase. Please contact the relevant retailer's support team to initiate a return.

How to Return an Item

  1. Go to the retailer's website where you made your purchase and log in to your account.
  2. Find your order in the "My Orders" or "Order History" section.
  3. Request a return by selecting the item and choosing a return reason.
  4. Follow the retailer's instructions — you may need to print a label or drop off at a courier location.
  5. Track your refund — most platforms process refunds within 3–10 business days after receiving the returned item.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items typically cannot be returned:

  • Digital products and software
  • Perishable or consumable goods
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items damaged after delivery due to customer misuse

Always check the product's return eligibility on the retailer's website before purchasing.
