How to return items or request a refund

Home / Returns

⚠️ Important Notice

MeowMeow does NOT process returns or refunds. All returns must be handled directly with the seller (Amazon, Daraz, Temu, AliExpress, etc.) where you made your purchase.

How to Return an Item

  1. Log into your account on the seller’s website.
  2. Go to “My Orders” or “Purchase History”.
  3. Select the item and click “Return or Replace Item”.
  4. Follow the instructions to print a return label (if applicable).
  5. Ship the item back using the provided method.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are typically processed within 3–5 business days after the seller receives your return. It may take additional time for your bank or payment provider to reflect the refund.

Exchanges

Most sellers allow exchanges for different sizes, colors, or models. Select “Exchange” instead of “Return” during the return process.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons, including:

  • Personal care items (e.g., beauty, grooming tools)
  • Intimate apparel
  • Opened software or digital downloads
  • Custom or personalized orders
Tip: Always check the seller’s return policy BEFORE purchasing. Look for “Free Returns”, “30-Day Window”, or “No Questions Asked” labels.
